Example 10. Pourbaix Diagram

Poubraix diagram for copper.

A Pourbaix diagram is a Eh-pH predominance area diagram. Such a diagram shows possible stable (equilibrium) phases in an aqueous electrochemical system. Note that the diagram dependens on temperature, pressure, and concentration. Pourbaix diagrams are commonly made at 10−6 M.
