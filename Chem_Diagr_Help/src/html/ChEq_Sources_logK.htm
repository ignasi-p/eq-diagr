Chemical Equilibria

Sources of Equilibrium Constants

You may get information on sources of equilibrium constants by searching the WWW. The following list contains some sites that, hopefully, may be helpful. Some of these sites offer free downloadable software which include databases with equilibrium constants.

  • USGS geochemical software:  water.usgs.gov / Phreeqc
  • Thermochimie  (www.thermochimie-tdb.com)  and Thermoddem  (thermoddem.brgm.fr)
  • JAEA:  migrationdb.jaea.go.jp
  • NAGRA-PSI:  www.psi.ch/les/software-and-database
  • MINTEQA2:  www2.epa.gov/exposure-assessment-models/minteqa2
  • VsualMINTEQ:  vminteq.lwr.kth.se
  • IUPAC Stability Constants Database:  www.acadsoft.co.uk
  • NIST Critical Stability Constants (SRD 46):  www.nist.gov/srd/nist46.cfm
  • MINEQL+ chemical equilibrium software: www.mineql.com
  • EQ3/6 software: missions.llnl.gov/energy/technologies/geochemistry

It is also possible to obtain equilibrium constants from thermodynamic data.
